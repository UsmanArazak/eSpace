Welcome to eSpace

eSpace is an organization affiliated to Dalhatu bn Ubaidullah,This organization is dedicated to providing opportunities for students and the community in the field of technology. This organization started its activities in late 2021, and the main goal of this organization is to bridge the gap between students and technology in this era
